Bavarian Cream Sponge Cake

Make this beautiful Bavarian cream sponge cake for someone special. A classic European dessert featuring a light sponge cake layered with rich, velvety Bavarian cream.

45 min

Prep Time

30 min

Cook Time

10

Servings

Ingredients

Sponge Cake

  • Flour - 250 grams (9 oz)
  • Butter - 80 grams (3 oz)
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • Sugar - 250 grams (9 oz)
  • Vanilla sugar - 2 teaspoons

Bavarian Cream

  • Milk - 150 ml (5 fl oz)
  • Heavy cream - 650 ml (22 fl oz)
  • Salt - a pinch
  • Sugar - 80 grams (3 oz)
  • 5 egg yolks
  • Vanilla sugar - 1 teaspoon
  • Gelatin sheets - 15 grams

Simple Syrup

  • Sugar - 100 grams (3.5 oz)
  • Water - 100 ml (3.5 oz)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Put 4 eggs, 3 yolks, sugar, and vanilla sugar in a bowl. Heat it over simmering water and stir the whole time.
  2. Move the mixture to a mixer and whip it on high for around 10 minutes until fluffy. Add sifted flour and fold gently.
  3. Mix some batter with melted butter, then fold it back into the rest.
  4. Pour batter into a lined 24 cm cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes. Check with a skewer to see if it is done.
  6. Cool the cake and take it out of the pan.
  7. Soak the gelatin in cold water.
  8. Make the syrup by boiling sugar and water together. Let it cool.
  9. Mix egg yolks and sugar together in a bowl.
  10. Heat milk, cream, salt, and sugar in a pot. Slowly add that into the egg yolks while stirring.
  11. Put it back on heat and stir until thicker. Add vanilla sugar.
  12. Melt the gelatin and stir it into the warm mixture. Let it cool some.
  13. Whip cream until stiff and fold it into the mixture.
  14. Slice sponge cake into 2 layers.
  15. Put one layer into the pan and brush with syrup.
  16. Spread most of the Bavarian cream on top.
  17. Place second cake layer on top and spread remaining cream. Chill for at least 5 hours.
  18. Draw chocolate lines on parchment paper if you want to decorate it like the example.
  19. Wrap the chocolate around the cake after it cools.
  20. Carefully take off the parchment paper.
  21. Pipe frosting around the edges and decorate the top.
