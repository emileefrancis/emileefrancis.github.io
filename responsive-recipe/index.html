Bavarian Cream Sponge Cake

Make this beautiful Bavarian cream sponge cake for someone special. A classic European dessert featuring a light sponge cake layered with rich Bavarian cream.

45 min

Prep Time

30 min

Cook Time

10

Servings

Ingredients

Sponge Cake

  • Flour - 250 grams
  • Butter - 80 grams
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • Sugar - 250 grams
  • Vanilla sugar - 2 teaspoons

Bavarian Cream

  • Milk - 150 ml
  • Heavy cream - 650 ml
  • Salt - a pinch
  • Sugar - 80 grams
  • 5 egg yolks
  • Vanilla sugar - 1 teaspoon
  • Gelatin sheets - 15 grams

Simple Syrup

  • Sugar - 100 grams
  • Water - 100 ml

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix eggs, yolks, sugar, and vanilla sugar.
  3. Heat it over simmering water and stir.
  4. Whip it until fluffy.
  5. Fold in flour gently.
  6. Add melted butter.
  7. Pour into pan.
  8. Bake 30 minutes.
  9. Cool cake.
  10. Make Bavarian cream and syrup.
  11. Layer cake and cream.
  12. Chill for 5 hours.
  13. Decorate and serve.
