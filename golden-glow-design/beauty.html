The Golden Glow beauty mood

This beauty style is not about looking overly done. It focuses on fresh skin, soft bronze tones, cream products, and hair that feels touchable instead of stiff.

Skin First

A simple routine with cleanser, moisturizer, sunscreen, and one treatment product is easier to keep up with than a complicated shelf full of products.

Warm Makeup

Cream blush, champagne highlight, brown mascara, and bronzy eyeshadow create a soft golden-hour effect.

Easy Hair

Lived-in waves, soft layers, and honey or caramel dimension fit the relaxed Golden Glow look.

Beginner glow routine

1

Prep the skin

Cleanse, moisturize, and use SPF during the day. Healthy skin makes every makeup look better.

2

Even out lightly

Use a skin tint or concealer only where needed so the final look still feels natural.

3

Add warmth

Use cream bronzer and blush to bring color back into the face.

4

Finish soft

Use brown mascara, lip balm, and a tiny bit of highlight on the high points of the face.
