Learn how UX works in fashion websites

This site is a beginner-friendly guide for people interested in fashion, branding, and web design. It explains how user experience shapes online fashion spaces and why good planning matters before a site is ever built.

The goal is to connect fashion and UX in a way that feels clear, visual, and useful for future creators.

Why this site exists

Fashion websites are not just supposed to look pretty. They also need to help people browse, understand products, and move through the site without frustration. This resource breaks those ideas down in a simple way.

Who it is for

This site is made for young creatives, especially college-aged users, who care about fashion, digital design, and branding. It is meant for beginners who want practical examples and not a bunch of complicated jargon.
