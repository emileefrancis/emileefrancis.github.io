Starter guide

Use this page as a quick plan for creating a Golden Glow routine without trying to change everything overnight.

Step 1: Choose your focus

Pick one category first: beauty, fashion, self-care, or organization. Starting small makes the project easier to keep.

Step 2: Make a basics list

Write down what you already own before buying anything. A glow-up does not have to start with a huge shopping list.

Step 3: Build a routine

Create one morning habit and one night habit. Keep them simple enough to do on a busy class day.

Step 4: Add personality

Choose details that feel like you: a favorite color, scent, necklace, hairstyle, playlist, or outfit formula.

Budget tips

  • Shop your closet before buying new clothes.
  • Buy basics before trend pieces.
  • Choose multi-use makeup like cream blush or bronzer.
  • Spend more on items you use every day.
  • Save inspiration photos before making purchases.

Mini challenge

For one week, pick three habits: one beauty habit, one outfit habit, and one self-care habit. Track how they change how you feel, not just how you look.
