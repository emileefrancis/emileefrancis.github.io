From Mood Board to Website

A long-scrolling story about turning a fashion idea into a real website.

Step 1: The Idea

Maya wanted to create a fashion website, but at first all she had was a folder of outfit photos, color ideas, and screenshots from brands she liked.

Step 2: The Mood Board

She started by organizing her ideas into a mood board. The colors, fonts, and images helped her understand what kind of feeling the website should have.

Step 3: The UX Plan

Before designing the pages, Maya thought about the user. She wanted the site to be stylish, but also simple to move through without getting confusing.

Step 4: The First Draft

She built a basic homepage with a title, navigation, images, and short sections. It was not perfect yet, but it finally looked like a real website.

Step 5: The Launch

After testing the links and fixing the layout, Maya published the site. What started as a messy mood board became something people could actually click through and use.
